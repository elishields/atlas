Moving the View Click and drag the background panel with your mouse.
Viewing Employee Information Click on an employee's card to view their information.
Zooming Hold control and use mouse scroll bar; or Hold Windows Key (Command Key on Mac) and use + or - keys.
Resetting Tree 'Reset to Top' will set the tree to be focused on the employee at the top of the organization. 'Reset to User' will set the tree to be focused on the current ServiceNow user.
